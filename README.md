## Hi, I'm Paulina Quintanilla

I am an **Assistant Professor** in **Process Systems Engineering** in the Department of Chemical Engineering at University College London (UCL), working on the modelling, control, and optimisation of dynamic systems.

---

## 🔬 Research interests

- Model Predictive Control (MPC) and Economic MPC  
- Data-driven and hybrid modelling (Physics + Machine Learning)  
- Gaussian Processes for control and optimisation  
- Digital twins for process systems  
- Mineral processing (flotation, SAG milling, leaching)  
- Smart and autonomous manufacturing systems

---

## 🎓 Education
- PhD in Earth Science and Engineering at Imperial College London
- MSc in Chemical Engineering at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María
- MEng in Chemical Engineering at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María
